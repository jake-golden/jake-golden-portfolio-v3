Who am I?

Hi! I'm Jake, and I design and build hardware, especially in the biomedical space. Right now that means analog board design and testing at National Instruments, plus personal projects spanning instrumentation, signal processing, and controls. Long-term, I'm chasing an R&D role building hardware for medical devices: wearables, diagnostic imaging, implantables, neural interfaces, surgical robotics.

On here you'll find some of my favorite personal projects and internship experiences — take a look around!
